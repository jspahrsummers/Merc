Merc is a top-down (2.5D), massively multiplayer space sim inspired by the Escape Velocity series of computer games, and more recently Endless Sky.

%UNITY_WEB_NAME%

Instructions:

  • Arrow keys to maneuver
  • Space to fire
  • M to show the galaxy hyperspace map
  • J to jump to hyperspace
  • L to land on a nearby planet
  • Scroll wheel to zoom in/out

There's a game server that you can connect to at merc.jspahrsummers.com. Try to destroy the idle frigate in the area... or other players. (If the game server is unavailable, it is probably just restarting to update to a new version, and will be up soon.)

Windows, Mac, and Linux versions of the game are also available for download on itch.io.
